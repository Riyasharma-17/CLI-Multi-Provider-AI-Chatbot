Q: What is recursion?
A: Recursion is a technique where a function calls itself to solve smaller versions of the same problem.

---

Q: What is a closure?
A: A closure is a function that remembers variables from its enclosing scope even after the outer function has finished executing.

---

Q: What is polymorphism?
A: Polymorphism allows different objects to respond to the same method in their own way.
